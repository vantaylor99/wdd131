My Method

I find it really difficult to find time for everything I'd like to do. I have found a lot of peace in choosing to try and put God first, make sure I give Him a piece of my time before anything else, After that, it's all about using my time wisely and avoiding distractions. Here are a few simple habits that help me stay balanced.

Give God Time First

Sometimes it can be tempting to put off scripture study.

Plan Your Week

I like to set aside time on Sunday to look at my week. It helps me stay focused and prevents surprises. Find a routine that works for you and stick with it.

Don't get stuck!

It's easy to lose time on things that don't really matter. Notice when you're stuck ‚Äî and move on. Even 10 minutes saved here and there adds up.

Social media time limits!

I set a 30-minute daily limit on social media. It's just enough to check in, without getting lost. Boundaries help you make room for better things.

If you're tired, get up earlier!

I know this sounds backwards ‚Äî but waking up earlier (even after a short night) sometimes helps me reset and get more from my day. Try it a few times and see what works for you.
